How it works

Three steps to gasless

1

Connect your Seeker wallet

Käte connects to your Seed Vault wallet via Mobile Wallet Adapter. Your keys never leave your device.

2

Deposit USDC for gas

Add USDC to your Gas Balance. $1 USDC covers approximately 4,000 Solana transactions.

3

Use any Solana dApp

Send, swap, and interact with any Solana app. Fees are deducted in USDC. No SOL ever needed.
